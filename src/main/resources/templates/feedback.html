About Us

Welcome to the Business Analyzer project website! We are a dedicated team of software engineering students, passionate about leveraging data to empower small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs). Our mission is to provide business owners with an intuitive, robust platform that transforms operational data into actionable insights. By simplifying data analysis, we aim to enhance decision-making processes and drive business profitability. Our team consists of experienced developers and data analysts committed to creating innovative solutions tailored to the needs of SMEs.
